Awareness

Women united, can never be divided

Women's awareness is the level of understanding and knowledge that women have about their rights, roles, and responsibilities in society. It involves being informed about issues that affect women, such as gender inequality, discrimination, and violence against women, and knowing how to advocate for themselves and others

Women empowerment

Together we rise, together we empower

When women are aware of their rights and the barriers they face, they can work towards achieving equality and making positive changes in their communities. Increasing women's awareness can be achieved through education, training, and access to information. This can involve providing resources and opportunities for women to learn about their rights and how to assert them, as well as encouraging dialogue and community engagement to promote awareness and action.
